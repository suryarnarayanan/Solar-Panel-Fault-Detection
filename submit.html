Cleaning Process


Follow these steps to clean bird droppings from solar panels effectively:


Wet the affected areas
Spray or apply the cleaning solution onto the bird droppings, saturating them to help loosen the debris.


Gently scrub
Use the soft-bristled brush or sponge to gently scrub the bird droppings, working in a circular motion to lift them off the panel surface. Avoid applying excessive pressure to prevent damage.


Rinse thoroughly
Rinse the panels thoroughly with water to remove the loosened debris and cleaning solution. Ensure that all traces of bird droppings are completely washed away.


Dry the panels
Use a clean microfiber cloth or squeegee to dry the panels thoroughly and remove any remaining water spots or streaks. Ensure that the panels are completely dry before reactivating the solar panel system.
