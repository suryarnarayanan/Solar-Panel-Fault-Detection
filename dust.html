Cleaning Process


Follow these steps to clean dust from solar panels effectively:


Keep a solar panel cleaning toolkit ready with all the tools, like the brush, wiper, etc. Dip the sponge brush in the water and gently rub the panels. It is advisable not to scrub the panels too hard. Harsh scrubbing will ruin the surface of the panels.


Another crucial point is to clean solar panels only by making them wet, or the dust particles stuck on them can bring scratches.

While cleaning the roof, using caution is necessary because the roof will definitely get slippery. Hence, it is advisable to use harnesses and safety equipment.


Do not use harsh chemicals or detergents to clean solar panels. This might cause damage to the solar panels.
