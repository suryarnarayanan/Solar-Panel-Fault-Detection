Cleaning Process


Follow these steps to setup solar panels effectively from electrical damage:


Safety First
Prioritize safety by turning off the system to prevent electric shock and wear appropriate protective gear, such as insulated gloves and safety glasses.


Conduct a thorough visual inspection to identify any visible signs of damage, including burnt wires, damaged connectors, or blackened areas.


Use a multimeter to test the voltage output of the solar panels and check for irregularities or fluctuations that may indicate electrical issues.


Address minor damage by repairing or replacing damaged components, such as wires, connectors, or junction boxes, following manufacturer guidelines.


After repairs, reconnect the panels to the system and monitor its performance to ensure the effectiveness of the repairs and prevent future damage.
